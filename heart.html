About

When I turned 21, I had spent exactly half my life in India and half in the US (neither continuously.) I'm slightly older now, and I live in Cambridge (MA), so... it's a delicate balance. When I think of home, I usually think of Pune, and of hills, traffic, and मिसळ!!!! But sometimes when I think of home, I think of Cambridge, and of the river, of colorful murals, and the energetic hustle of the universities nearby.

I love trying to do things that I don't really believe I can do. I do not like doing things that I don't want to do. However my bar for wanting to do something is not terribly high. Two truths and a lie: my sleep schedule is unreasonably precious to me, my Spotify Wrapped '22 features 22 languages, and I'm actually so bad at lying I just don't try.

Volunteer opportunities that I recommend

  • Tarjimly: on-demand interpretation for refugees
    If you are multilingual, you can volunteer anytime to translate for refugees, using just a smartphone app
  • MIT Educational Studies Program
    Teach Anything to middle-school or high-school students at one of these annual MIT-student-run programs.
    If you're not located near MIT, check out Learning Unlimited for similar programs or to start your own chapter.
