Rujul Gandhi

[ऋजुल / rʊdʒʊl]


I'm studying how our brains make sense of speech! Currently, I work as a research associate in the Chang Lab at UC San Francisco. I've been using artificial neural networks to model neural activations in response to spoken language, and working on making the results of this emerging paradigm more interpretable.


In 2023, I finished an M.Eng. at MIT where I implemented natural language processing systems for robots at the Reliable Autonomous systems Lab. Prior, I completed my S.B. in linguistics and EECS at MIT.


I enjoy thinking about language(s), being on the move, engineering, and putting together projects with teams of people. Other significant interests include rowing (shoutout Pacific Rowing Club), co-operative living, and Indian classical dance.
